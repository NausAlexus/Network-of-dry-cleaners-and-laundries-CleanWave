Frequently asked questions

Find out more about us!

In the CleanWave FAQ section, we have collected answers to the most popular questions from our customers. We understand that laundry and dry cleaning services can raise many questions, and we strive to provide you with all the information you need to ensure your comfortable service.

  • 1. How do I book a dry cleaning service?

    • To book a dry cleaning service, simply visit our CleanWave website or use the mobile app to select the services you need and fill out the order form.

  • 2. What documents do I need to use laundry services?

    • No special documents are required to receive our services. Just bring your items, and our specialists will take care of everything.

  • 3. What is the cost of dry cleaning services?

    • The cost of services depends on the type and number of items. You can find current prices on our website or in the CleanWave app.

  • 4. In which areas do you operate your laundries and dry cleaning services?

    • Our locations are spread throughout the city. You can find information about the nearest CleanWave branch on our website.

  • 5. How do I pick up my items after dry cleaning?

    • You can pick up your items at the CleanWave location you specified. We also offer delivery services for your convenience!

  • 6. What types of fabrics do you accept for dry cleaning?

    • We accept a wide variety of fabrics, including delicate and heavy materials such as silk, wool, and synthetics. If you have any doubts, our specialists are always here to help.

  • 7. Can I use dry cleaning services for my wedding dress?

    • Yes, we offer professional handling services for wedding dresses to ensure they look as good as new. We care about your memories!

  • 8. How can I contact customer support?

    • You can reach our customer support through the "Help" section on our website or by calling the number provided on our site.

  • 9. How quickly will I receive my items after dry cleaning?

    • We typically complete dry cleaning within 24-48 hours, depending on the volume of work. We will always inform you about the completion time of the service.

  • 10. Can I store my items for an extended period?

    • Yes, we offer long-term storage services for your items in a clean and secure environment. You can inquire about this at our branch.
