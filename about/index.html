Our Core Values

At CleanWave, sustainability is at the heart of everything we do. We believe that caring for the environment is not just a responsibility but a necessity. That’s why we use biodegradable detergents, energy-efficient machines, and water-saving technologies in all our processes. Our goal is to minimize our carbon footprint while delivering top-notch cleaning services. By choosing CleanWave, you’re not just getting clean clothes—you’re contributing to a cleaner, greener planet.

Customer Focus

Our customers are our greatest asset. We are committed to providing exceptional service that exceeds your expectations. Our customer support team is always ready to assist you with any queries or concerns. At CleanWave, your satisfaction is our top priority, and we go the extra mile to make sure you’re happy with our services.

Excellence

We strive for excellence in every aspect of our business. From the quality of our cleaning processes to the professionalism of our team, we set high standards and consistently deliver on them. How We Work

Schedule

Booking a pickup with CleanWave is quick and easy. Simply use our app or website to select a date and time that works best for you. Our user-friendly interface ensures that you can schedule a pickup in just a few clicks. Whether you need a one-time service or a recurring appointment, we’ve got you covered. Our flexible scheduling options make it convenient for you to fit laundry into your busy life.

Pickup

Once you’ve scheduled a pickup, our team will arrive at your doorstep at the designated time. We understand that your time is valuable, so we ensure punctuality and professionalism at every step. Our drivers are trained to handle your garments with care, and they will provide you with a receipt for your items. You can trust us to take care of your clothes as if they were our own.

Clean

Your clothes are cleaned using the latest technology and eco-friendly products. Our state-of-the-art facilities are equipped with advanced machines that ensure a thorough and gentle clean. We use biodegradable detergents that are tough on stains but gentle on fabrics. Whether it’s dry cleaning, washing, or stain removal, we handle every garment with the utmost care and attention to detail.

Deliver

Once your clothes are cleaned and pressed, we deliver them back to you within 48 hours. Our delivery team ensures that your garments are returned in pristine condition, neatly packaged and ready to wear. We offer flexible delivery options, including same-day delivery for urgent orders. With CleanWave, you can enjoy the convenience of premium laundry services without ever leaving your home.
